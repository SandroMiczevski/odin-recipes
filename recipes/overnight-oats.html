Overnight Oats

Ingredients

  • 50g Oats
  • 120g Almond milk - or other option of vegetable milk
  • 15g Peanut butter - better if it is powder
  • 20g Whey protein - Any flavour of your taste
  • 10g Chocolate drops

Instructions

  1. Pour the oats in a jar or mug and the milk, mix it well.
  2. Add the peanut butter and whey protein, and mix it well
  3. Add the chocolat drops
  4. Put it into your fridge and leave it Overnight.
